About Us

From a folding table in the back yard to golf balls delivered right to your hole

How We Started

It began as a small stand set up in our back yard, selling golf balls to neighbors and anyone passing by. There was no grand plan behind it — just a simple setup and a steady stream of golfers who needed a ball or two. We liked doing it enough to keep going, and eventually decided it was time to expand beyond the back yard.

That decision led us to bring the same idea online. Instead of walking up to a folding table, customers can now place an order from anywhere on the course and have golf balls brought straight to their hole. The setup has changed, but the goal is the same one we started with: make it easy to get a golf ball when you need one.

What We Do Today

Today, our website lets golfers order golf balls in seconds — just enter your name, your hole number, and how many you need. Orders come straight to our team, and we get them out to you quickly so your round doesn't have to stop. We're still a small operation at heart, just one that's grown past the back yard.

  • Started as a golf ball stand in our back yard.
  • Grew through word of mouth on the course.
  • Expanded into a full online ordering system.
  • Orders go straight to our team in real time.
  • Golf balls delivered right to your hole.
